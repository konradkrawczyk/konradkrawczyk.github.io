About Me

I work on the interface of academia and pharma industry, specializing in bioinformatics of antibodies. I am an Assistant Professor at the University of Southern Denmark and a founder of Natural Antibody

You can find more about my research interests below.

Antibodies

As a specific microcosm of protein-protein recognition, I look at antibodies -- our organisms way to develop a 'binder' against an arbitrary molecule. I am Interested in how antibodies achieve their binding and how they can be engineered to create better therapeutics, focusing mainly on their structure. This effort is chiefly summarized on my website antibodystructure. My current goal here though is to put structure on the next generation sequencing data, which is summarized by my website antibodymap.org.

Protein-Protein interactions

I am Interested in how proteins recognize each other as this is what runs the show in our organisms. In this directions please see my current website proteincontact.org
